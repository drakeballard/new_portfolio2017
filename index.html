ABOUT ME

My name is Drake Ballard and I am a multi-cultural individual. I was born in the U.S. but grew up primarily in Germany and a little bit in Costa Rica. Soon after high school, I moved to Texas and attended Texas State University - San Marcos and obtained a B.A. in Business Administration focusing on Management. After a few years working in the corporate world, I decided that I want to transition my career to something more meaningful and decided to become a Full Stack Developer. My goal and aspirations are to run my own business in the near future. In the meantime, I want to obtain an opportunity in the Tech Space and be a great asset to the development team by providing my skillset as Full Stack Developer, but also integrate my business background. In my free time, I like to spend my days outdoors and partake in activities such as Surfing, Snowboarding, Snorkeling, Mountainbiking and various other activities.
